Delete Your HelepApp Account

If you want to permanently delete your account and all associated personal data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Email us at support@helepapp.tech with your account details (full name, phone number, email used for registration).
  2. Specify that you want your account and data deleted.

Once we receive your request, we will delete:

  • Personal information (name, phone, email)
  • KYC verification documents
  • Service requests, booking history, and chat messages

Some data, such as transaction records, may be kept for legal or tax compliance purposes (up to 5 years).

If you have any questions, contact support@helepapp.tech.
